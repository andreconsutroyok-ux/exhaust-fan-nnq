Our Way of Doing Things

We stick to a plan that has a few parts to it, so we know we're being responsible and staying green:

**1. Research and learning:** We start by trying to get the heart of the matter -- looking at the challenges and rooting around for some smart ideas.

**2. Map it out:** From A to Z, we put together a plan to pinpoint what we want to do and mix it with what we already want to have done eventually.

**3. Put it all together:** We get down to biz by making sure our plans are doable, easy, and worth it in the long run.

**4. Set it in motion:** We have a sharp eye for making sure we dot the I's and cross the T's in our project deliverable.

**5. Keep a close watch:** The trick is to ask 'was this effective? Did this project help make a change?'

Each little piece of our method is designed to be mindful, well done, and on point.
